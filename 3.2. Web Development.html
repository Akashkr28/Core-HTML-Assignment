Web Development

Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites or web applications. It encompasses a wide range of tasks and technologies to ensure that websites function effectively and provide a positive user experience. Some key aspects of web development are:

  1. Front-End Development,
  2. Back-end development,
  3. Full-Stack Development,
  4. Web Development Framework,
  5. Responsive Design,
  6. Testing and Debugging,
  7. Security,
  8. content management system,
  9. Deployment and Hosting.

Web development is a dynamic and in-demand field, playing a crucial role in enabling businesses, organization and individuals to establish an online presence and deliver content and services to a global audience. It requires a combination of technical skills, creativity, and problem solving activities.
